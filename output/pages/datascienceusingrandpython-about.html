About

This blog is documenting my journey to improve my data mining skills using freely available data. Feel free to post your comments and/or questions below each blog entry.

Me- My name is Crystal and as the title would suggest, I live in Seattle.

My Job - I have a PhD in Human Genetics and Genomics. I am currently mining data on health care records and OMIC data to improve diagnosis

This Blog - The goal of this blog is to answer some interesting questions and hopefully find some others out there that have similar interests. Feel free to post your comments and/or questions below each blog entry.
